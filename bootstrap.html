In America, more than 20% of the melons we grow never make it off of farms because they aren’t perfect enough for grocery stores’ standards. This results in billions of pounds of melons going to waste every year. We’re talking about melons that are just as nutritious, and delicious as perfect melons that get rejected just because they look a little different. With so many delicious melons going to waste every year, we knew we had to do something to make an melon-sized-impact.

As a college student, our CEO, Ivan Ivanovich, noticed that a lot of melons were going to waste in the cafeteria at the University of Ohio. To address this problem, he founded the SnapMelon, a for-profit dedicated to preventing waste of melons across the country. Since its founding, SnapMelon has expanded to over 180 countries and continents.
